TV Mount Types

  • Swivel
  • Tilt
  • Full 360

Swivel Mounts are best used for where you want the TV to be viewable from two different Angles, such as a Living Room / Dining Room. You can swivel the TV while you eat and turn it back while back in the living room.

Tilt mounts are limited in the Right to Left motion but offer a limited Up and Down tilt. These mounts comes in Slim profile to get a real cool picture frame look. These mounts are recommended for Fireplaces as it allows the TV to be tilted down for an optimal viewing angle.

These mounts are usually used in Businesses and are attached to the Ceiling - not to the wall. This type of Ceiling mount allows for a full 360 rotation along with tilt.
